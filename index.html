Who We Are

James J. Seok CEO

James J. Seok is the CEO of vital smith. He studied medical engineering and business from Korea university. Starting from 2013, Exito, a medical marketing company has successfully marketed medical equipment as well as medicine. We have learned extensively about engineering and marketing through these business experiences. Vital Smith vows to produce innovative products with the help of outstanding medical workers

Aiden Hwang CFO

Aiden Hwang is the CFO of vital smith, He majored in Business Economics from UCLA, He supervises the activities of the subsidiary companies in the US, as well as financial management at Vital Smith, we aim to globalize Korean medical advancements and expand to care for third world countries

Ian Shim Creative Director

He is the one of co-founder of vital smith, He majored in industrial design and consulting from hansung university He was awarded the winner from 2013 red dot design award, He has acquired experience from global design award He wants to communicate with many people by creating a product that will create a healthy society.

Jacob Jung Creative Director

He majored in industrial design from Hansung university. He has received many outstanding awards such as IF design award, IDEA design award. His goal is to contribute to the medical industry by inventing and developing idea in the medical field based on variety of design experience such as Samsung design membership, co-op program

Ki Hoon Nam Director

Ki Hoon Nam is in charge of the medical side of Vital Smith. He has graduated from Korea University's Medical school, and attained his medical license in 2014. From 2012, for a period of one year, Nam has served as the Chairperson for the Korean Medical School Student Association, and has developed an extensive network in the medical field. He is currently also serving as KI RA's director of public relations. It is his belief that doctors should be closely involved in the development process of medical equipment to ensure the superior products and that this should be the driving motivation of Vitalsmith.
